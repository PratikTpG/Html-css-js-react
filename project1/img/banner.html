About us

Namlo Himalaya is a non-governmental organization registered in Social Welfare Council of Nepal. It carries out social projects in the least benefited schools and communities of Nepal.

See more

What we do

We strive to make a positive and sustainable difference in education and livelihood of people through its projects helping communities to become self sufficient and without creating dependency.

Projects

Namlo implements range of projects related to the  improvement of school and communities such as school construction, school development, school training, scholarship and sustainable community development.

Our current project

 

Construction of two girl's toilets in Tahanun District

During 2023, Namlo supported two schools in Tahanun district to construct female toilets. These toilets will provide proper facilities to accommodate around 800 girls from Kyamin and Tribhuwan School.
